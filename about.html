About SharkFinder India

Our Mission

At SharkFinder India, we're passionate about connecting Indian consumers with the innovative products featured on Shark Tank India. Our mission is to make discovering and purchasing these unique products as easy as possible, while supporting the entrepreneurial ecosystem in India.

What We Do

We compile comprehensive information about every product that appears on Shark Tank India, organized in an easy-to-search format. Our database includes details about the entrepreneurs, their pitch, the deals made with the Sharks, and most importantly - where you can buy these products.

Our Story

SharkFinder India was created by an avid fan of Shark Tank India who wanted to make it easier for others to discover and support Indian entrepreneurs. After watching each episode and being inspired by the innovative products, our founder realized there was no single platform where viewers could easily find and purchase these products.

That's why we built SharkFinder India - to bridge that gap and help both consumers and entrepreneurs connect. By providing affiliate links to these products, we're able to maintain this free resource while supporting the businesses that have appeared on the show.
